Ran Ding

I am an MSc candidate in Informatics at the Technical University of Munich and a student researcher at the Visual Computing and Artificial Intelligence Lab, led by Prof. Matthias Niessner. Previously, I earned a BEng in Computer Science, from Dalian University of Technology.

Under Prof. Zhenyu Wu and Prof. Xu Jia's supervision, I concentrated on robotics and machine learning as an undergraduate. During my senior year, I was chosen to be an exchange student at TUM, where I began my research and interest in 3D computer vision.

My current research focuses on 3D reconstruction in computer vision. More specifically, I am working on shape reconstruction and generation, non-rigid deformation, neural implicit representation.
